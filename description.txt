Adds extra variants of the dirt bomb that explode and create other blocks such as snow, stone and mud. Useful for patching holes while building or creating custom terrain.

Below is a list of blocks this mod adds regular and sticky bomb variants for:\
[list]
[*]Snow
[*]Ash
[*]Mud
[*]Stone
[*]Sand
[*]Cloud
[*]Ebonstone
[*]Ebonsand
[*]Pearlsand
[*]Pearlstone
[*]Crimstone
[/list]
